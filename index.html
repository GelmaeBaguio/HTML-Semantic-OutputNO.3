About SMCC

Saint Michael College of Caraga also referred to by
its acronym SMCC is a private, Roman Catholic, basic education
and higher education institution run by the Roman Catholic Diocese
of Butuan in Nasipit, Agusan del Norte in the Philippines.
It was established in 1948 by the Missionaries of the Sacred
Heart (MSC) fathers. Its main campus is located at Atupan
Street, Barangay 4 Poblacion, Nasipit, Agusan del Norte. The
second campus is located in Brgy. Triangulo houses the
elementary department.
